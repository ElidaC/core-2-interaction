Pier 57

From a clothing swap to a climate discussion, Pier 57 is

celebrating Earth Day with a variety of festive activities

bringing together climate enthusiasts, local organizations, and

community members of all ages.









This event is free and open to everyone interested in making a

positive impact on our planet. While there's no cost to

participate, we'll be accepting donations at the event to

support our community initiatives.







Welcome!

Veggie Mijas


A collective of BIPOC individuals advocating for food and

environmental justice by creating safe spaces for sharing

plant-based lifestyle experiences, fostering community

through an intersectional lens.


Climate Cafe


A community of climate activists dedicated to nurturing

connections and promoting actionable outreach in support of

the climate movement.


Black Girl Environmentalist


A national organization dedicated to addressing the pathway

and retention issue in the climate movement for Black girls,

women, and gender-expansive people.


Remake


A global advocacy organization advocating for fair pay and

climate justice within the clothing industry.


Reclypt


A hub that explores how we create change in the circular

economy through events & activations.


Worn Not Torn


The NYC branch of an anti-capitalist clothing swap community

that is founded on sustainability, creativity, & the idea of

getting dressed as a healing art practice, with the goal of

collective community care & rebellion against the

consumerist values of the fashion industry.


We Are Atlas


Inspired by the Greek God, Atlas (who held Earth on his

shoulders all alone), W.A.A invites everyone to acts of Real

Love, Simple Action meant to heal Earth & improve our

collective legacy.
