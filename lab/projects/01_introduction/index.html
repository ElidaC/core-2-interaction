Why Elida???



I'm a Chinese girl who grew up in a Christian family.

When my parents asked me to choose an English name,

I was fascinated with the name "Elijah,"

both for the pronunciation and the meaning.

Since it's a boy's name,

I changed it a little bit to combine "Alida" and the prefix "Eli" together,

and it becomes "Elida."
